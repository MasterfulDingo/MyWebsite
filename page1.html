Goblins: A Summary

Goblins are small and sneaky buggers. While weak in straight combat, they will often set numerous traps to incapacitate or disarm adventurers who dare enter their lairs, before overwhelming them and taking their gear and loot as trophies to decorate their dwellings with.

Runesmith's take
