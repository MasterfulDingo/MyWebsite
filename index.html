This website was made by Lachlan Row in 2019 for the NCEA level 1 achievement standard 91880, based around creating a media outcome. This site is my outcome, and uses the following techniques.

  • Styling to make a sticky topbar, with links to other pages that change colour if they are active or being hovered over
  • Importing a number of fonts from an external folder in .otf formats and using them in various parts of my website in positions fitting of the style of the fonts
  • Using borders, margins and padding to succesfully position divs where I want them on the screen, even when the view port size is changed.
  • Linked videos from youtube to the site and linked them to the theme
  • Given images borders and subtitles, allowing users to more fully understand the contect of the images
  • Created a cohesive and aesthetically pleasing theme for the website, fitting for it's content.

I also attempted to include a scrolling sidebar with the sticky attribute as well, but I could not figure it our after a solid week of attempts, and so decided to leave it out.

I hope you enjoy this website, and appreciate the effort I have put into it.
